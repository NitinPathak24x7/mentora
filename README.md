🌐 Mentora – A Digitalized Mental Health Solution

Welcome to Mentora, a safe and stigma-free space designed to support students in higher education and high schools who are facing challenges like stress, anxiety, depression, burnout, insomnia, and social isolation.

🎯 Our Purpose:
To provide a structured, scalable, and accessible psychological intervention system that ensures early detection, prevention, and support for mental health concerns.

🤝 What You’ll Find Here:

Guidance from trained psychology mentors

A judgment-free environment to share and learn

Awareness and coping strategies for mental health issues

A step towards reducing underutilization of counseling support due to fear, lack of options, or accessibility barriers

💡 Remember: You are not alone. This community is built to help you heal, grow, and thrive.
